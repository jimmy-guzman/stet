## Switch themes

Press `t` to open the theme switcher: filter by name and move (or hover) to
preview the whole UI live, `enter` to apply, `esc` to revert. stet ships `dark`
and `light` built-in themes, plus an all-grey `mono-dark` and `mono-light` pair
it starts on when `NO_COLOR` is non-empty, and follows your terminal's
appearance by default.

![The theme switcher listing themes with color swatches, previewing the highlighted one live](/screenshots/theme-switcher.png)

The switch lasts the session only. To make a theme permanent, or to define your
own, set it in your [configuration](/docs/reference/configuration), where a theme
can inherit another with `base` and override individual tokens or its `syntax`
highlighting.
